AlinGo – Terms & Conditions

1. Platform Role

AlinGo is a technology platform that connects sellers, service providers, riders, and customers. AlinGo does not own, prepare, or deliver food or goods.

2. Commission Policy

A standard 2.5% commission is charged on each completed transaction. This applies to sellers, managers, drivers, logistics partners, and service providers.

3. Seller Responsibilities

  • Food quality and hygiene
  • Correct pricing
  • Timely preparation
  • Legal licenses and permits

4. ID & Performance System

Each partner is assigned a unique AlinGo ID. Accounts may be promoted, restricted, or blocked based on performance, ratings, or policy violations.

5. Payments & Settlements

Payments are processed through digital wallets. Settlements are issued weekly or on-demand after commission deduction.

6. Suspension & Termination

AlinGo reserves the right to suspend or terminate accounts in case of fraud, misconduct, or repeated complaints.

7. Legal Compliance

Partners are solely responsible for complying with local laws, food safety regulations, and business licensing requirements.
